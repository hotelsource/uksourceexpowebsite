UK Sourcing Expo 2026 is Uttarakhand's first dedicated B2B trade fair — designed for manufacturers, suppliers and brands who want to enter the North India market, appoint dealers, or connect with institutional buyers across hospitality, construction, healthcare and industry.

Unlike national trade fairs where you compete with 3,000 exhibitors, UK Sourcing Expo has 100 focused stalls — meaning every visitor sees your brand. The Uttarakhand market is underserved, fast-growing and actively seeking suppliers. This is the fastest route in.

FAQ

Common Questions

Who should exhibit at UK Sourcing Expo 2026?
Manufacturers, importers, brands and dealers looking to appoint distributors or find new buyers in Uttarakhand and North India. Especially relevant for companies in kitchen equipment, construction, tiles, sanitaryware, medical equipment, safety, packaging and interior categories.
Who attends as visitors?
Hotel GMs, restaurant owners, builders, contractors, hospital purchase managers, school administrators, factory purchase heads and institutional buyers from across Uttarakhand — Dehradun, Haridwar, Rishikesh, Mussoorie, Roorkee, Haldwani and beyond.
Is MSME finance really available on-site?
Yes. SBI, SIDBI and Bajaj Finance will have representatives at the expo. Buyers and dealers can apply for equipment loans and get in-principle approval within the expo. This means your dealer can fund their first order on the spot.
How many stalls are available per sector?
Each sector has between 8 and 20 stalls. Once a sector is full, we do not add more exhibitors — keeping the buyer-to-exhibitor ratio high and ensuring each exhibitor gets meaningful footfall.
How do I book a stall or register as visitor?
