Frequently Asked Questions

What is Source India?
Source India is a national trade intelligence platform analyzing B2B sourcing patterns, manufacturing hubs, dealer network development, and institutional procurement trends across India's major industrial cities and emerging markets.
Which manufacturing hubs are covered in this analysis?
The analysis covers Delhi NCR, Mumbai Metropolitan Region, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, Ahmedabad, Morbi, Ludhiana, and emerging markets including Uttarakhand's Selaqui Industrial Area, Himachal's Baddi-Paonta Sahib, and Chandigarh Tricity region.
How is this intelligence useful for manufacturers?
Manufacturers use this intelligence to identify untapped dealer network opportunities, understand regional procurement behaviors, assess competition intensity, and make informed decisions about market entry strategies in emerging Tier-2 and Tier-3 markets.
What sectors are analyzed in depth?
The platform covers eight primary sectors: Construction Equipment & Building Materials, Commercial Kitchen & HORECA Equipment, Medical & Hospital Equipment, Sanitaryware & Plumbing, Industrial Packaging & Food Processing Machinery, Safety & Fire Equipment, Gym & Fitness Equipment, and Interior Design & Furnishings.
How frequently is the data updated?
The platform continuously tracks sourcing patterns, trade show participation, dealer appointment announcements, and institutional procurement trends. Major comprehensive updates are published quarterly, with real-time alerts for significant market developments.
Can this intelligence help with dealer network planning?
Yes. The platform identifies geographic gaps in dealer coverage, quantifies demand potential by region, analyzes competition intensity, and provides market entry frameworks for manufacturers seeking to establish regional distribution partnerships.
